Research Interests

  • Algebraic geometry and number theory (with a geometric flavor).
  • ℓ-adic local systems; ℓ-independence; Fontaine–Mazur conjecture; Simpson’s motivicity conjecture.
  • Local systems over mixed-characteristic local fields; arithmetic properties over deformations of stable curves in the Deligne–Mumford stack.
